## Top Recommendations for the Next Expansion

### 1. Phylum **Annelida** (Segmented Worms) — *~22,000 species*
* **Ecological Importance:** Extremely high. Essential soil aerators, nutrient cyclers, and benthic marine foundation species.
* **Major Classes / Groups:**
  * *Polychaeta* (Deep-sea hydrothermal tube worms, Christmas tree worms, bristle worms)
  * *Clitellata* (Earthworms and leeches)
* **Bootstrap path:** `scripts/scoutPhylum.ts Annelida` → `scripts/cacheGbifClass.ts <class>` → `scripts/importClass.ts`.

### 2. Phylum **Onychophora** (Velvet Worms) — *~200 species*
* **Full Curation Candidate:** Small species count → can be 100% complete + Wikipedia-enriched in one pass.
* Similar profile to how Ctenophora was wrapped up.

### 3. Phylum **Porifera** (Sponges) — *~8,500 species*
* **Visual Appeal:** Reasonable — glass sponges and tropical demosponges photograph well.
* **Ecological Importance:** Foundation species in many reef systems.
